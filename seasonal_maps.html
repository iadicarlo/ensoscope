About These Maps

Each figure is shown for a single ENSO event-type/intensity selection. Observed maps (CHIRPS) are based on daily precipitation 1981-2025, regridded to 0.25°. CESM2 LENS patterns use approximately 100 ensemble members over a detrended historical period, providing a large-sample estimate of the forced ENSO signal.

Product descriptions

  • RX1day Anomaly vs Neutral - seasonal-max daily rainfall anomaly relative to the neutral-phase composite.
  • RX10day Anomaly vs Neutral - 10-day accumulation maximum anomaly relative to neutral.
  • RX1day / RX10day Absolute - mean values (mm) without anomaly baseline removal.
  • Probability Ratio - P(above-normal extreme | ENSO phase) / P(above-normal extreme | Neutral). Values > 1 indicate elevated risk; < 1 indicate suppressed risk.
